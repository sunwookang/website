DJ Inquiries

I am an up-and-coming wedding DJ in the Seattle area.

I started off DJing in 2014 as a student volunteer for my high school. I eventually started getting gigs for other high school dances in the Seattle area through Bust-A-Move DJ Services. I kept doing this until I recently decided to individually transition into DJing weddings.

Venturing off into this journey has been a pretty confusing experience, so I thank you for being patient with me and for helping me provide you with the best dance party of your life. I look forward to working with you!

Your experience

This is your special day. I am here to listen to your requests and do my best to provide the experience that you deserve. Whether it's the specific YouTube cover of your favorite song or a microphone set up for a live performance, I am here to cater to your needs and to prepare the necessary equipment for a quality time.

Equipment

I use a Pioneer DDJ-SB2 controller along with Serato DJ to mix the music live. I use two QSC K12.2 speakers as the main audio outputs. They have built in woofers that help pack a punch and works well with most medium sized weddings. For larger weddings, I will rent more speakers and a subwoofer.

Pricing

With my wedding DJ career just starting, my rates are accordingly lower than your average wedding DJ. I want to be upfront about that with my customers. However, this lower rate is solely because of the lack of experience and is not representative of any lack of skill. I can assure a quality music and audio experience.
